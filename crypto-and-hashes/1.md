**Types of cryptography:** <br><br>
**There are two types of cryptography systematric and asymmetric. Symmetric cryptography uses one shared key for both encryption and decryption, while asymmetric cryptography uses a public key to encrypt and a private key to decrypt for stronger security.**
<br><br>
**Questions:** <br>
**1) What type of cryptography is more secure?** <br>
**-> asymmetric**
<br><br>
**2) What type of cryptography is faster?** <br>
**-> symmetric**
<br><br>
**3) What type of cryptography will a Bank site use?** <br>
**-> asymmetric**
<br><br>
**4) What will you use to encrypt your messages in asymmetric cryptography?** <br>
**-> public key**
<br><br>
**5) What will you use to decrypt messages in asymmetric cryptography?** <br>
